This app allows you to set up automatic transmission of necessary parameters of a specific deal to Google Analytics in 4 steps when an event occurs: win, loss, or transition to a specific stage. Based on the events transmitted to Google Analytics, you can generate the necessary reports in the future.

Parameters that are most frequently transmitted in events:

  • deal budget
  • reasons for loss
  • stage from which the loss occurred
  • stage to which the deal moved
